Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

This Return and Refund Policy explains the refund process for OMaa Planner iOS app subscriptions and in-app purchases. Please read this policy carefully to understand how refunds are handled.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Important: OMaa Planner is distributed through Apple's App Store. All purchases, including subscriptions and any in-app purchases, are processed exclusively by Apple. OMaa does not process payments or refunds directly.

When you purchase a subscription or make an in-app purchase through the App Store:

  • The transaction is processed by Apple, not by OMaa
  • Your payment method on file with Apple is charged
  • Apple maintains records of your purchase history
  • All refund requests must be submitted to Apple

Refund Policy for Digital Subscriptions

Digital subscriptions are generally non-refundable once the service period has begun.

This is a standard App Store policy that applies to all iOS app subscriptions.

However, Apple may grant refunds at their discretion in certain circumstances, such as:

  • Accidental or unauthorized purchases
  • Technical issues that prevent you from using the app
  • Purchases made by minors without parental consent
  • Other circumstances as determined by Apple

How to Request a Refund from Apple

Apple Handles All Refunds

Since all purchases are made through the App Store, you must contact Apple directly to request a refund. Apple's support team will review your request and determine eligibility based on their refund policies.

To request a refund from Apple:

Option 1: Report a Problem Online

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find the purchase you want to request a refund for
  4. Click "Report a Problem" next to the item
  5. Select the reason for your refund request
  6. Submit your request

Option 2: Contact Apple Support Directly

  1. Visit support.apple.com
  2. Select "Billing & Subscriptions"
  3. Choose "Request a refund"
  4. Follow the prompts to submit your request

Option 3: Through Your Purchase History

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Media & Purchases
  4. Tap View Account (you may need to sign in)
  5. Tap Purchase History
  6. Find the purchase and tap it
  7. Tap Report a Problem

Canceling Your Subscription

Canceling your subscription is different from requesting a refund. When you cancel:

  • You will retain access to premium features until the end of your current billing period
  • Your subscription will not renew at the end of the period
  • You will not receive a refund for the remaining time in your current billing period

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select OMaa Planner
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Reminder: Cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date to avoid being charged for the next billing period.

Free Trial Refunds

If you are on a free trial:

  • You will not be charged during the free trial period
  • If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged
  • If the trial converts to a paid subscription and you were charged unintentionally, you may request a refund from Apple

What OMaa Cannot Do

Please understand that OMaa:

  • Cannot process refunds directly - All payment processing is handled by Apple
  • Cannot access your payment information - Apple manages all financial transactions
  • Cannot override Apple's refund decisions - Apple has final authority on refund requests
  • Cannot extend subscription periods - Subscription management is controlled through your Apple ID

Technical Issues

If you are experiencing technical issues with the app that prevent you from using your subscription:

  1. Contact us first at support@omaa.mom
  2. We will attempt to resolve the issue
  3. If we cannot resolve it, we can provide documentation to support your refund request to Apple
